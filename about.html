About Sambalpur University Startup Forum

Sambalpur University StartUp Forum (SUSF) is effort taken by Sambalpur University, Jyotivihar Burla, Sambalpur, Odisha to start an Incubation cum Skilling Centre to bring the students, faculties, entrepreneurs and corporates of Western Odisha to come under one roof to innovate, research, develop business solutions as per the need of the society.

Sambalpur University was established in1966 and recognized by the University Grant Commission. It is a member of the Indian Association of Universities as well as the Commonwealth Association of Universities. Sambalpur University has been accredited by NAAC with A Grade in September 2016 for a period of five years. It has 20 Post-Graduate Departments, 1 Constituent Law College, 1 Constituent Engineering College, A Directorate of Distance Education and a number of centres like Private Education Cell, UGC Human Resource Development Centre (HRDC), an Academic Staff College. The University also offers a number of Self Financing Courses. Besides, there are one hundred and forty* affiliated colleges under the University. The University has envisioned being a centre of excellence in higher education with a focus on innovative teaching, learning, research, consultancy and extension activities for building a creative, enlightened and productive civil society.

SUSF has been registered as a NOT FOR PROFIT organization under the Sec - 8 of Companies Act, 2013 since Sept ,2021

Objective of SUSF
  • To promote entrepreneurship among students, faculties and people in and around Western Odisha.
  • To identify, nurture business ideas and entrepreneurs and promote them with knowledge, product building, funds and infrastructure.
  • To build a conducive environment for the students, faculties, entrepreneurs and people from the corporate to continually come together and work for better business solutions as per the need of the society.
  • To explore partnership opportunities with Corporates, Social Organizations, Institutions in identifying problems and finding solutions,
  • To train amateure entreprenuers in various legal and non legal compliances and formalities required to develop their products and business.
